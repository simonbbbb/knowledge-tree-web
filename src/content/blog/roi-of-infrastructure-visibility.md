## Making the Business Case

Infrastructure visibility tools are an investment, and like any investment, you need to justify them with numbers. This framework gives you the quantifiable metrics to make the case to your CFO, CTO, or board.

## The ROI Framework

### Component 1: Time Saved on Documentation

**Formula:** Engineers × Hours/month × Loaded cost/hour × 12

**Example (50-person infrastructure team):**
- Current state: 40 hrs/month per team on manual documentation
- With Knowledge Tree: 2 hrs/month (review, not create)
- Savings: 38 hrs/month × $75/hr = $2,850/month per team
- **Annual: $34,200 per team**

### Component 2: Faster Incident Response

**Formula:** Incidents/month × Hours saved × Engineers involved × Loaded cost/hour × 12

**Example (15 P1/P2 incidents/month):**
- Current MTTR: 4.2 hours, with 5 engineers involved
- With Knowledge Tree: 1.7 hours (60% reduction)
- Savings per incident: 2.5 hrs × 5 engineers × $75/hr = $937
- **Annual: $168,660**

### Component 3: Reduced Compliance Costs

**Formula:** Audit cycles/year × Weeks per audit × Engineers involved × Loaded cost/hour × Hours/week

**Example (2 SOC 2 audits/year):**
- Current: 8 weeks × 3 engineers × $75/hr × 40 hrs = $72,000/audit
- With Knowledge Tree: 1 week × 1 engineer × $75/hr × 40 hrs = $3,000/audit
- **Annual savings: $138,000**

### Component 4: Decommissioned Waste

**Formula:** Orphaned resources discovered × Average monthly cost

**Example:**
- Discovered: 3,200 orphaned resources across 23 AWS accounts
- Average cost: $45/resource/month
- **Annual savings: $1,728,000** (typical: $150K-$500K for mid-size enterprises)

### Component 5: Reduced Onboarding Time

**Formula:** New hires/year × Weeks saved × Loaded cost/hour × Hours/week

**Example (20 new hires/year):**
- Current onboarding: 12 weeks to infrastructure proficiency
- With Knowledge Tree: 3 weeks (graph teaches the system)
- Savings: 9 weeks × 40 hrs × $75/hr = $27,000 per hire
- **Annual: $540,000**

## The Total Picture

| Component | Annual Savings |
|-----------|---------------|
| Documentation time | $34,200 |
| Faster incident response | $168,660 |
| Compliance costs | $138,000 |
| Decommissioned waste (conservative) | $180,000 |
| Reduced onboarding time | $540,000 |
| **Total** | **$1,060,860** |

Against a Knowledge Tree Business plan cost of ~$60,000/year (20 seats), that's a **17x return on investment**.

## Conservative vs. Aggressive Estimates

Even using the most conservative numbers (halving every estimate), the ROI is still **8x**. And these calculations don't include harder-to-quantify benefits like:

- Reduced risk of security breaches from unknown infrastructure
- Faster architecture decisions with complete dependency maps
- Improved engineer satisfaction (less time on tedious documentation)
- Better capacity planning with real resource utilization data

## Presenting to Leadership

When presenting this to your leadership team, lead with the headline number: **"For every $1 we invest in infrastructure visibility, we get $17 back."**

Then back it up with the component breakdown. Most CFOs will immediately focus on the decommissioned waste number — it's the most tangible. The incident response and compliance savings are the most defensible.
